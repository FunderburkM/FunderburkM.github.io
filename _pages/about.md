Greetings, I'm Mario :D. I reside in the Greater Vancouver Area, Canada, and I am a game and software developer. As an avid fan of technology, computer hardware and software, I became interested in video game development and virtual content generation. I started working with Game Engines in 2014, Unreal Engine being my primary engine.  

Starting with Level and Blueprint Scripting, started generalizing myself into the engine in 2016 and began working with C++ in 2017. I have worked on singleplayer, multiplayer, and virtualization applications for Windows and Android. My most prevalent areas of knowledge and expertise inside Unreal are Gameplay, mid-low level systems, networking, application development, animation, physics and particle effects, materials (in-engine and HLSL), and have mid-level knowledge of 2D and 3D content generation.  

Outside of Unreal Engine, I have also worked in front-end web development, python, and application processing with pascal. My interests include psychology and neuroscience personal studies, as well as physics, trigonometry, and calculus. On my free time, I find enjoyment in video games, film and anime, and entertaining a black ball of fur known as Lobo.
